history of Egypt :

Egypt is considered one of the oldest human civilizations in the world, as the history of Egypt dates back to before 3100 BC, and the beginning of the state of Egypt was established on the banks of the Nile River, and the Egyptian civilization continued for more than two thousand years, as the history of Egypt is the most long-lived and extended history among other civilizations throughout the world.
The ancient Egyptian civilization added a lot to the human heritage around the world. The Nile Valley witnessed the establishment of the first central authority in history, in addition to the knowledge of writing and the contribution to the innovation of many sciences, including arithmetic, engineering, medicine, astronomy, knowledge of the calendar, and thinking about resurrection after death, reward and punishment, which prompted the kings of Egypt to build amazing temples and tombs scattered throughout All over Egypt, the most important of which are the pyramids, which are the most important and largest tourist symbol in Egypt and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
In addition to knowing the methods of embalming the bodies of the dead, the secrets of which are still unknown until now. Egypt passed through several historical stages after that, during which it was subject to the rule of the Greeks, Persians and Romans until a new birth emerged for Egypt with the entry of Islam and its spread in all its lands.
